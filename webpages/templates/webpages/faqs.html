Faqs

Can I exchange or return a poster?

We offer 28 day exchanges or returns on all our posters. If in case for any reason you're not happy with your purchase. If it's a refund we'll check the item to make sure it's still in good condition before we issure the refund. We recommend to keep the box for 28 days just in case you change your mind. We provide the paid return label as well, so you dont have to worry about the postage.

How long before I a receive my order?

We usually ship your order within 24 hours of receiving it. The delivery times are usually 3-5 days within the UK and 1-2 weeks internationally. For international deliverys there may be additional delay because of the couriers.

Does the poster come with a warranty?

We have a warranty on printed posters of 1 year. But we do not offer a warranty on drawing, painted posters etc. as they are made by hand.

How do I hang up my poster?

All our frames come with a mount so you can hang your new poster without drilling any holes. We will soon be offering frames as well.
